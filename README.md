# 📊 Oil & Inflation Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between crude oil prices and inflation across multiple countries using Power BI. The goal is to identify trends, patterns, and correlations that help understand how oil price fluctuations impact inflation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze trends in oil prices and inflation over time
* Compare inflation across different countries
* Identify the relationship between oil prices and inflation
* Build a clean, interactive dashboard for business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* Excel/CSV Dataset

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Metrics

* Average Oil Price
* Average Inflation Rate
* GDP Growth
* Interest Rate
* Maximum Oil Price

### 🔹 Visualizations

* **Line Chart** → Oil Price vs Inflation trend over time
* **Scatter Plot** → Relationship between Oil Price and Inflation by country

### 🔹 Interactivity

* Country slicer
* Reset filter button
* Dynamic filtering

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Oil prices and inflation show a positive relationship over time
* Increase in oil prices generally leads to higher inflation
* Countries respond differently, indicating varying economic sensitivity
